GRCC Programmers Club!

GRCC Programmers Club is dedicated to students with an interest and enthusiasm for

Computer Programming and Computer Science. Our goal is to expand programming

education at GRCC by hosting fun and educational coding competitions, informational

workshops for students to learn practical applications for the workplace, and attending local

events related to Computer Science. Join us this semester on Mondays and Thursdays!
