GRCC Programmers Club!

GRCC Programmers is a club is dedicated to students with an interest and enthusiasm for Computer Programming and Computer Science. Our goal is to expand programming education beyond the classroom by hosting fun and educational coding competitions, informational workshops, and having fun attending local events related to Computer Science.
